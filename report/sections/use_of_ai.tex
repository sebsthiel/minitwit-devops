\section{Use of Generative AI}

AI tools such as ChatGPT and other LLMs have been utilized for assisting with code-developing. At the start of the project, LLMs were included as co-authors when they were used for code development. However, as the project progressed, this was not done consistently and overlooked. LLMs have also been used for debugging, and to better understand how certain technologies work. For instance ChatGPT was used to pinpoint the differences in docker-compose files, depending on whether they were used in swarm mode. Additionally LLMs have been used to validate grammar throughout the report. Overall AI tools have been used from time to time as a sparring partner.
